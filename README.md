Project Queries

Top 3 performing categories per quarter
Identify high-revenue categories by season to plan inventory and promotions.

Revenue share by category per month
Measure each category’s contribution to total revenue.

Top 10 customers by lifetime value (LTV)
Highlight most valuable customers for loyalty rewards and retention programs.

Months with highest revenue by category
Detect peak months for each category to plan campaigns.

AOV of new vs repeat customers
Compare spending behavior to focus on acquisition vs retention.

Month-over-month growth by category
Track revenue growth trends across product categories.

Customers with 3-month spending increase
Identify customers with consistent growth for targeted offers.

Profit margin variance by city
Rank stores based on profitability.

Outlier customers (spend > 2× average)
Detect unusually high spenders for further investigation.

Products with 3-month decline in quantity
Spot products losing traction to adjust pricing or stock.

Products with 3-month declining sales
Measure continuous sales drop for root cause analysis.

Business Insights

Seasonal demand drives profit in top categories.

Repeat buyers sustain consistent revenue.
